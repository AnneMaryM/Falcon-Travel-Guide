    Travel Tips

    Welcome to Fisher College!

    Moving into campus is an exciting time for any student, but it can also be a bit overwhelming. We are here to provide you with some helpful travel tips to make your transition into college life a little smoother. Whether you're moving from another state or just down the street, these tips will help you get settled in and enjoy your time at Fisher College to the fullest.

    1.    Pack wisely: When packing for your move, it's important to consider what you'll actually need on campus. While it may be tempting to bring everything you own, try to stick to the essentials to make your move-in process easier. Also, make sure to label all of your boxes with your name and room number to make sure they arrive at the right place.
    2.    Plan your travel: If you're coming from out of state or need to book a flight, make sure to plan your travel ahead of time. Check for any travel restrictions, book your tickets early, and consider using a transportation service to get you to campus.
    3.    Check in with your roommates: If you're living with roommates, reach out to them before you move in to coordinate who's bringing what. This will help you avoid duplicates and make the most of the space in your room.
    4.    Familiarize yourself with the campus: Take a virtual tour of the Fisher College campus before you arrive to get an idea of where everything is located. This will make it easier to find your classes, the dining hall, and other key locations on campus.
    5.    Connect with other students: Moving to a new place can be lonely at first, but don't be afraid to connect with other students to make new friends. Join clubs or organizations, attend events, or even just strike up a conversation with someone in your residence hall.

    Moving into campus is an exciting time for any student, but it can also be a bit overwhelming. We are here to provide you with some helpful travel tips to make your transition into college life a little smoother. Whether you're moving from another state or just down the street, these tips will help you get settled in and enjoy your time at Fisher College to the fullest.
