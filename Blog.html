    How Is It Like To
    Be A Falcon

    To Our Future Falcons


    Many students join Fisher every year, all from different backgrounds and with a different experiences, but they are all aiming to build a better future and have a good career. We brought to you some of the Falcons to talk about there lives before and after Joining Fisher.

    I started my Fisher journey in 2019, and it has been the best 4 years of my life. I have met a lot of people from different backgrounds and I learned a lot from them.

    Fisher brought to me a lot of opportunities. While focusing on my career and goals I can still enjoy my hobbies and develop my skills.

    My experience at Fisher College was life-changing. I joined the college hoping to become a professional basketball player, and it exceeded my expectations. The college offered me many opportunities that helped me achieve my dreams. Fisher College truly gave me a unique and valuable experience that I will always be grateful for.


    Fisher College is a place where students can not only receive a quality education but also have the opportunity to grow and develop as individuals. With a diverse community, ample opportunities for personal and career development, and a supportive environment, students can pursue their passions and achieve their goals. As highlighted by the experiences of Carla, Mark, and Chiana, Fisher College can truly be a life-changing experience, providing a valuable foundation for a successful future. To our future Falcons, we welcome you to embark on your own journey at Fisher College and look forward to the great things you will accomplish.
