    CELEBRATE THE JOURNEY

    Moving to Fisher College and the vibrant city of Boston can be an exciting experience for students. Many students enjoy attending events around the city and meeting new people. Exploring the city's art, music, and cultural scenes can be a fun way to get to know the city and make new connections. Fisher College is located in the heart of Boston, giving students easy access to a variety of events and activities. For those looking to broaden their horizons and enjoy new experiences, attending events in Boston can be a great way to do so.
