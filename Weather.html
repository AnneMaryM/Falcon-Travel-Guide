    Weather

    Weather at Fisher College

    Fisher College is located in Boston, Massachusetts, and as such, the weather can be quite unpredictable. It is important to be prepared for all kinds of weather when attending Fisher College, so be sure to check the forecast before you pack for your trip.

    What to Bring for Cold Weather

    During the winter months, temperatures can drop to freezing or below, so it is important to bring warm clothes to stay comfortable during your time at Fisher College. Here are some things you may want to consider packing:
    • A warm winter coat
    • A hat and gloves
    • Scarf
    • Thermal underwear or long johns
    • Warm socks and boots

    What to Warm for Cold Weather

    Boston can experience hot and humid summers, so be sure to pack accordingly. Here are some things you may want to consider bringing:
    • Light, breathable clothing such as shorts, t-shirts, and sundresses
    • Sandals or flip-flops
    • Sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun
    • A reusable water bottle to stay hydrated

    Other Essentials to Remember

    In addition to packing for the weather, there are other essentials you may want to consider bringing to Fisher College, including:
    • A backpack or messenger bag to carry your books and supplies
    • Your laptop or tablet for taking notes and completing assignments
    • A portable phone charger in case your phone battery runs low
    • An umbrella or rain jacket in case of rain
    • A water-resistant bag or cover for your backpack in case of rain or snow


    By being prepared for the weather and bringing the essentials, you can make the most of your time at Fisher College without worrying about the weather or forgetting something important. We hope this guide helps you pack for your trip and enjoy your time at Fisher College!.
