    A Step By Step Guide

    To A Better Experience

    My Goal


    I have started this project to help the new students settle down in there new homes without having to go through the struggles that we have been through. MY only aim was to ease down the journey because I know how hard it can be when you are starting all over again.
    Who Am I ?

    I spent a year studying at Fisher and I must say, it was an incredible experience. The faculty and staff were very supportive and always willing to help. The courses were challenging yet engaging, and I felt like I was really learning and growing as a student.
    ~System Analysis and Design Project

    I had the plesure of working on this project during the Spring 2023 semester in my System Analysis and Design class. Luckily, the project I chose was a breeze to select and create since I had gone through the process before when I enrolled at Fisher College. It was nice to work on something familiar, and my prior experience made the process smoother and more enjoyable.
