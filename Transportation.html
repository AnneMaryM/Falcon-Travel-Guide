    Transportation

    Are you lost? You can't find your way? Thankfully all the roads take you to Fisher...

    Find your way to fisher

    Fisher College has four locations, with Boston being the flagship campus. Located at the intersection of Beacon and Arlington Street there are several different commuting options to get you from home to Downtown in no time.

    Directions by Car

    Directions by Car
    From I-95, US 1, or Route 3

    Follow signs to Boston via Interstate 93. Take Exit 26 (Storrow Drive/Cambridge) and proceed to the Copley/Back Bay exit (on the left). At the end of the exit ramp, turn right at the traffic signal onto Beacon Street.

    Directions by Public Transportation

    The Boston Campus is located just a short walk from the Arlington Street station on the Green Line. Exit the Arlington Street station to the left. At the top of the stairs, make a hard right onto Arlington Street. When you reach Beacon Street, cross the street, and turn left. Fisher College is located at 118 Beacon Street.
    Take the Orange Line to the Downtown Crossing stop. Exit and walk southwest on Hawley Street toward Summer Street. Turn right onto Summer Street, walk straight towards and through the Boston Common until reaching and turning left onto Beacon Street.

    Directions by Commuter Rail

    Directions by Commuter Rail

    Boston's Back Bay Station is served by Amtrak and MBTA Commuter Rails originating from the west and south of Boston. Once inside the station, exit and walk towards either Dartmouth or Clarendon Street.
