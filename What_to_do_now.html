    A What To Do Now

    Explore Boston

    As a new comer to Boston, you'll find that the city is full of history, culture, and entertainment. From walking along the Freedom Trail to exploring the many museums and galleries, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city. We've compiled a list of some of the top attractions and activities to help you get started on your Boston adventure. Whether you're looking to explore the city's history, experience its nightlife, or simply take in the stunning views, there's plenty to keep you busy.

    Take a walk along the Freedom Trail and learn about the city's history at landmarks such as the Massachusetts State House, Paul Revere's House, and the USS Constitution Museum.
    Spend a day exploring the museums in Boston, including the Museum of Fine Arts, the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum, and the New England Aquarium.
    Take a tour of Fenway Park and cheer on the Boston Red Sox
    Head to Harvard Square and explore the campus of Harvard University and the quaint shops and cafes in the area.
    Visit the historic Beacon Hill neighborhood and enjoy the architecture and charming streets.
    Take a walk or bike ride along the Charles River and enjoy the beautiful views of the city.
    Check out the nightlife in the city, with plenty of bars and restaurants to choose from in areas such as Faneuil Hall, the North End, and the Seaport District.

    We hope this guide has given you some ideas for how to make the most of your time in Boston as a new student at Fisher College. This city has so much to offer, and we encourage you to explore as much of it as possible. Whether you're interested in history, art, culture, food, or entertainment, Boston has something for everyone.
